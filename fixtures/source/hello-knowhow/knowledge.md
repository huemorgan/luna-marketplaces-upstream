# Cultural Greetings Knowledge

When greeting someone, consider their cultural context:

- **Japan** — Bow slightly; verbal greeting: "Konnichiwa"
- **France** — Light cheek kisses (la bise); verbal: "Bonjour"
- **India** — Palms together (Namaste); adjust formality by age
- **Brazil** — Warm embrace; verbal: "Olá"
- **Germany** — Firm handshake; verbal: "Guten Tag"
- **Middle East** — Right hand over heart; "As-salamu alaykum"

Always match the formality to the relationship and context.
